# Lab

Active **research & experimentation** — things being figured out before they earn a
place in Foundations or Components. Expect churn; APIs here are unstable.

Tag experiments `research` (and `experimental` once they're usable). When an
experiment graduates, it moves to its real home and sheds the `research` tag. This is
distinct from **Archive**, which is frozen old work, not forward-looking exploration.
