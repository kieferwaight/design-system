# Surface Primitive

<Description of={Stories} />

The `Surface` primitive is an altitudinal layout sheet panel extending `Box`. It integrates physical layout layers and glassmorphic translucent materials with spacing, border-radius, and z-axis shadow configurations.

## Key Scenarios

### 1. Altitudinal Concentric Nesting

When nesting containers, keeping card properties aligned and concentric is critical. By declaring the `level` property (0..6), the `Surface` automatically resolves outer-to-inner corner radius limits (`layer-0` to `layer-6`) and pairs them with calibrated background highlights and shadow layers:

<Canvas of={Stories.AltitudinalTiers} />

### 2. Premium Glassmorphic Materials

Employs native Apple-style translucent overlay blurs using GPU-accelerated backdrop-filters (`ultra-thin` up to `thick`), creating depth layers above complex canvases:
